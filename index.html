Po-Ming is a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science working with Dr. Rahul Basole in Georgia Tech Visualization Lab. His research interests lie in visual analytics, information visualization and human computer interaction.

He received a degree in Computer Science and a degree in General Business Management from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. Before joining Georgia Tech, He worked as an undergraduate researcher in HKUST VisLab.
