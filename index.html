ABOUT ME
I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science working with Dr. Alex Endert and Dr. John Stasko. I am a member of Georgia Tech Visualization Lab. My research interests lie in human-computer interaction and visual analytics.

I received a degree in Computer Science and a degree in General Business Management from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. Before joining Georgia Tech, I was a member of HKUST VisLab.
